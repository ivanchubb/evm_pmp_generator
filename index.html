Formulas
  • CV = EV - AC
  • SV = EV - PV
  • CPI = EV / AC
  • SPI = EV / PV
  • TCPI = (BAC - EV) / (BAC - AC)
Definitions
  • CV (Cost Variance): Measures cost performance in a project. Difference between earned value (EV) and actual costs (AC).
  • SV (Schedule Variance): Measures schedule performance in a project. Difference between earned value (EV) and planned value (PV).
  • CPI (Cost Performance Index): Measure of cost efficiency. CPI less than 1 indicates cost overrun of the estimated cost at a particular time.
  • SPI (Schedule Performance Index): Measure of schedule efficiency. SPI less than 1 indicates a delay in the project.
  • TCPI (To-Complete Performance Index): Indicates efficiency needed for the remainder of the project to meet a financial or schedule goal.
